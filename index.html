About Me

I didn't start in tech. I started in a warehouse, progressed into data and operations roles, spent two years in the NHS analysing workforce information, and eventually landed in legal tech — building electronic forms and writing VBA macros at One Advanced Ltd, where I still work today.

Somewhere in that ten years I realised I wanted to build the things, not just use them. So in 2022 I started a BSc in Web Design and Development at Edge Hill University. I graduate in summer 2026 with a focus on accessibility and UX research — because after years of working with software that didn't quite work for the people using it, that part matters most to me.

I'm looking for graduate or junior developer roles in Leeds from summer 2026.
